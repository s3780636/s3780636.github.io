My ideal job would be to work for video game giants such as Treyarch or Infinity Ward. The people behind my childhood favourite, Call of Duty. They both have a career section on their websites and the available position I found was at Infinity Ward. Currently I do not meet any of those qualifications except for having experience using the listed online services. I hope that the skills I obtain during university will be able to get me started with other programming languages. Although I would ideally like to be able to create my own games, learning the networking side to video games is also important to me. The position in this advertisement requires you to implement high performance networking code solution for both the client side and server side game logic and to make the games' network traffic as optimal as it can be.
