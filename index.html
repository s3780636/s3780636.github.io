Project Ideas.

I have not started any projects or really have proper ideas for some but I would like to eventually create my own game on Unreal Engine. I have downloaded it before and tried messing around with it but I was lost. I had no clue what to do. The reason why I would like to use Unreal Engine is because very popular games have been made with it and has been around for just over 20 years. Games such as Fortnite, Gears of War, Tekken 7, Mortal Kombat X and many more. Unreal Engine is written in C++ meaning I will have to learn how to write in C++ before I start making my own games in UE. I hope that the coding skills I develop during my time at university will help in learning C++. The main type of video games I would love to create are fighting games like Tekken or first person shooters like Call of Duty. It has always been a dream of mine to make a Call of Duty game. As there is a very large majority of the world who play games, I hope to appeal to them by creating a good game with enjoyable content or at least be part of a team of people who create a game together. Statistics show that there are 2.2 billion gamers in the world and counting meaning there is a large demand for video games. I do not expect all 2.2 billion to play a game I create, got to start small. Aim to please a minority, and the rest will follow. Video game popularity is increasing daily. Fortnite's Battle Royale mode took the world by storm, no pun intended, gathering a massive player base in such a short time. With brand new content every in-game season, it drew people in. Of those 2.2 billion it is estimated that almost half spend money while playing, imagine the money you could make if your game is successful. Unreal Engine is not the only game engine on the market but with its user friendly interface it is the one I am opting for. You cannot really use Unreal Engine on low spec machines and is highly recommended you have a high end machine that will be able to handle the stress put on it by the range of 3D effects it has to offer. Render times will be drastically reduced if you have top of the range hardware compared to if you had low end components. You do not want your computer crashing mid render, causing you to lose the progress you made. As Unreal Engine is written in C++, I will have to learn the basics of it to at least get a start on a video game. You can find simple written tutorials in Unreal Engine but looking online for video tutorials for Unreal Engine and C++ will be able to provide a better understanding and explain things clearer. Anime like Dragonball Z, Naruto and One Piece all revolve around fighting, cool transformations and flashy abilities and I would like to create a game involving those. Games with good story lines appeal to me more than games with a better multiplayer experience. I love putting on my headphones and feeling like I've become one with the game I am playing. Games need to have really good score, sound effects and visuals otherwise they can be quite mundane. My goal is to make sure I make every aspect of the game loved by the user. I have spent too much money on games where I felt needed more but I have also played many games where I could not stop, like Destiny. Using that experience, I will know what to implement in to my games and what to leave out. If one day my creation becomes successful I will not stop at one. I will continue to produce more content.
